TCS Interview Preparation Steps (Beginner Friendly)
1. Understand TCS Company
Learn about Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)
Know what the company does (IT services, software solutions)
Understand basic details like:
Headquarters (Mumbai, India)
CEO name (optional but helpful)
Learn about TCS hiring programs like TCS Ninja and Digital
2. Prepare a Good Resume
Keep your resume simple and clear
Include:
Name, contact details, email
Education details (BCA)
Technical skills (C, Java, Python, SQL, HTML, etc.)
Mini projects or internship work
Avoid unnecessary information
Keep it 1 page if possible
3. Improve Technical Knowledge
Learn basic programming:
C or Java fundamentals
Study important topics:
Data Structures (Array, Stack, Queue, Linked List basics)
Object-Oriented Programming (OOP concepts)
Practice SQL queries:
SELECT, INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE
Revise basic DBMS concepts
4. Practice Aptitude
Learn basic maths:
Percentage, Ratio, Profit & Loss
Practice logical reasoning:
Puzzles, number series, coding-decoding
Improve English:
Grammar, sentence correction, comprehension
Take online mock tests daily
5. Improve Communication Skills
Practice speaking English daily
Prepare self-introduction
Speak clearly and confidently
Avoid fear while speaking
Try speaking in front of a mirror
6. Prepare for HR Interview
Practice common questions like:
Tell me about yourself
Why do you want to join TCS?
What are your strengths and weaknesses?
What are your career goals?
Answer honestly and confidently
7. Do Mock Interviews
Practice with friends or teachers
Record your answers and improve mistakes
Simulate real interview environment
8. Final Preparation Tips
Revise basics every day
Stay calm during the interview
Dress neatly and professionally
Be confident and polite
Don’t panic if you don’t know an answer—try explaining logically
